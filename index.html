World Population In 1978

A brief visual depiction of the world in 1978

It is common known fact that India and China are the two most populus countries but was it always the case. As of 2018 as seen in the previous visulizations, there was a clear trend that India will be overtaking China in the comming years and smaller countries in terms of area like Sweden and Singapore did not even regiseter properly on the graph as the magnitude difference was extremely high. This visulaization tries to explore the situation fifty years back and give visual/Interactive perspective of the past

For this visualization the same countries were taken into consideration but the data was restricted to a single year - 1978
